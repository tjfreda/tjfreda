Lemon Grass & Chicken Summer Rolls

Servings: 16

  • 1/2 cup Ground Peanuts
  • 1/4 Cup Fish Sauce
  • 3 Tablespoons Lime Juice
  • 2 Teaspoons White Sugar
  • 1 Tablespoon Seasame Oil
  • 16 Rice Wrappers
  • 2 Cup Mint Leaves
  • 1/3 Minced Cilantro

1

Boil slightly-salted water in a large pot. Season the chicken with salt and cook in the boiling water, 7 to 10 minutes. Transfer to a large platter and allow to cool completely. Shred into small pieces.

2

Combine shredded chicken, ginger, jalapeno pepper, cucumber, basil, mint, cilantro, lemon grass, and peanuts in a large mixing bowl. Whisk together fish sauce, lime juice sugar, sesame oil, and peanut oil in a small bowl: add to the chicken mixture.

3

Fill a shallow pan with hot water. Dip the rice paper wrappers in the hot water until soft. Spread the rice paper onto a clean, flat surface. Place 2 leaf of the lettuce into the center of a sheet of rice paper: spreak about 1/3 cup of the chicken mixture onto the lettuce leaf. Roll into a cylinder. Repeat. Cut into halves to serve.
